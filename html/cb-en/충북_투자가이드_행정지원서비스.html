One-Stop Service for Foreign Investment

We are doing the ONE-STOP of Administrative Support when foreigners want to invest in our province.
  1. Foreign investors
  2. KOTRA Foreign Investment Support Center
  3. Director of Investment Attraction Division
  4. Foreign Investment Attraction Council
  5. Director of Business Promotion Support Division
  6. Foreign investors
  • 1. Request authorization and permission (complaint handling, etc.)
  • 2. Resolve minor complaints immediately
  • 3. Request processing (for KOTRA employees)
  • 4. Discuss with the city, county, and council regarding authorization and permission
  • 5. Send notification of results of discussion with the city, county, and council regarding authorization and permission
  • 6. Send notification of final results
