Financial Support

  • Employment subsidy
    ※ Chungbuk: within KRW　1 billion per business
    • Qualified business: In the case when companies hire 20 or more workers within five years from the date of FDI company registration
    • Amount of subsidy: KRW 100,000~500,000/month, within 6 months
  • Educational training subsidy
    ※ Chungbuk: within KRW 1 billion for each business
    • Qualified business: In the case when FDI companies hire 20 or more workers and provide them with educational training
    • Amount of subsidy: KRW 100,000~500,000/month, within 6 months

Cash Grant

  • Qualified business: business with over 30% of foreign investment share
  • Qualified business
    • High-level technology accompanying business or industrial support service business with investment of over USD 10 million
    • Foreign-invested companies that invest in parts, material and manufacturing industries with investment of over USD 10 million
    • Research institutes which hire more than 10 researchers with master's degree and more than three years of work experience
  • Use: lease or purchase of land, purchase of capital goods and establishment of infrastructure
  • Support range: 5%~15% of total investment amount (local budget) (Provision of leased land is included in cash grant)
