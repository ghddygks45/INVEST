Best Logistics Infrastructure

Equipped with a full transportation and logistics system including bullet trains (KTX), roads and airports and an industrial railroad that enters industrial complexes, Ulsan provides the best accessibility.
Railway transportation

With the help of the bullet train KTX, approximately two hours is all you need to reach Seoul and other major cities nationwide. Also, rail lines have been constructed within the industrial complex so it is convenient to transport large amounts of products or raw materials.

Air transportation

Ulsan Airport is located near the city center with regular daily flights to Seoul. Gimhae International Airport is only an hour drive away, providing access to a variety of international destinations for your convenience.

Road transportation

Four expressways and five national roads penetrate the city. They directly connect the industrial complexes with big cities like Seoul and Busan as well as downtown Ulsan.
