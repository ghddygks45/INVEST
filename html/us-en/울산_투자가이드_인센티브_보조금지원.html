Subsidies

Subsidies : Classification, Qualification, Support details
Classification Qualification Support details
Subsidies for Employment When employing more than 20 Ulsan residents that have lived in Ulsan city for more than six months within five years after registration as a foreign-invested company KRW 500,000 per extra employee every month for up to six months
Subsidies for Employee Training When training more than 20 new workers within five years after registration as a foreigninvested company KRW 500,000 per employee every month for up to six months limited to KRW 200 million per company
Subsidies for Facility Construction and Expansion When businesses with high technology or industry support services invest more than KRW 3 billion in facility construction or expansion Up to 2 percent of the amount spent over KRW 3 billion with a maximum limit of KRW 200 million per company (within assigned budgets)
