Superior Manpower

Ulsan, widely known as the youngest city in Korea with an average age of citizens being 35, is also well known for its abundant skilled manpower due to its 50-year-long history as an industrial city. Around 3,000 talented engineers in automobile, shipbuilding & marine, chemical, and environment fields graduate every year from Ulsan University, UNIST and 60 other nearby universities.
