FDI Status

158 foreign enterprises are investing USD 8 billion in Ulsan.
If you look at investment size by country, foreign investors in Ulsan are from 32 various countries, including the United States, Japan, the Netherlands, and Germany. Investment by European companies has been particularly active and among them, German companies have invested the most. Looking at investment by industry, traditionally FDI inflow has been concentrated on Ulsan’s core industries namely, automobile, shipbuilding & marine, and petrochemical industries. There has also been a robust FDI inflow into electric & electronic, energy, metal & machinery and service industries, signaling the growing attractiveness of Ulsan in more diverse sectors.
