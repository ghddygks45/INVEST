Cash Grants

Eligibility
The foreign investment ratio shall be over 30 percent and meet one of the following requirements.
  • Construction or addition of factory facility in order to operate an industry support service industry, high technology accompanying business, component and material business
  • Construction or addition of factory facilities that create new jobs that exceeds the number of newly employed regular workers by business type eligible for cash grants (300 jobs for manufacturing)
  • Has more than five researchers who possess a master's degree in the field of an industry-support service, high technology or bachelor's degree in related industry while having research experience for more than three years to construct or increase research facility for research and development activities for the business
  • In the case when a Foreign Investment Committee recognizes it deserves the support due to its highly influential nature on the local economy from huge investment
    • As a foreign company that operates business in more than three countries, establish local headquarters in Korea, which has jurisdiction over two countries
    • When running regional strategic industry or regional leading industry, it should be able to contribute to regional economic development
Support
Eligibility decision is made based on matters such as high technology, technology transfer, new job creation and duplication with domestic investment
Usage
  • Land purchase or rent for plants or R&D facilities
  • Construction costs for plant facilities or research facilities
  • Capital goods / research equipment purchasing costs for plant facilities or research facilities
  • Foundation facilities installation cost (electricity, communication, etc.) for plant facilities or research facilities
  • Funds to support employment and training
