Abundant Industrial Utilities

Ulsan has two power plants including Yeongnam Thermal Power Plant and Ulsan Thermal Power Plant with power capacity of 3.75 million kW that supply stable power to Ulsan and its neighboring areas.
The full capacity of industrial water is about 1.545 million ㎥ per day and the actual demand for water is only 60% of the full capacity. The city is well-equipped with essential industrial facilities such as sewage disposal, waste incineration plants and landfills.
