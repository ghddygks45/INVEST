Cash Grant

Cash Grant : Topic, Description
Topic Description
Support criterion The share of FDI should be more than 30%
Qualifications A. High-tech or industry-supporting service companies with FDI of over USD 10 million
B. Parts and materials manufacturers with FDI of over USD 10 million
C. R&D involving FDI of over USD 5 million and over 20 new employees with a master's degree or higher
Purposes Land purchase, construction, equipment purchase, employment, employee training & education, or infrastructure construction
Evaluation criteria Technology transter effects, job creation effects, locating in an underdeveloped area, not-overlapping with domestic investment
Support amount Negotiable within the amount of 5% of the total FDI
