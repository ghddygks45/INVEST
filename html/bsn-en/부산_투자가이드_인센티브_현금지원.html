Cash Support

Cash Support : Subsidy, Description
Subsidy Description
Education & training subsidy
  • For new employment of more than 20 employees, support KRW 100,000–500,000 per employee per month
  • Limit: A total of KRW 200 million (USD 200,000)
Employment subsidy
  • For new employment of more than 20 employees, support KRW 100,000–500,000 per employee per month in excess
  • Limit: A total of KRW 200 million (USD 200,000)
  • Period: within 6 months
Consulting support
  • Support up to 30% of consulting cost, including legal and accounting costs
  • Limit: A total of KRW 20 million (USD 20,000) / 1 time
