Daejeon, located in the center of South Korea, serves as the hub of transportation, distribution, and economy with the most dynamic industrial activities and economic development.
Daejeon, the hub city of Northeast Asia, is Korea’s best investment destination. With the world’s best science and technology, the city offers a very convenient metropolitan transportation network system with access to every region in Korea within 90 minutes.
As the host city of the World Technopolis Association, Daejeon cooperates with major countries and contributes to the development of the global industry through science and technology.
In particular, Daejeon has received a credit rating of Aa2 from Moody’s, an international credit-rating agency. It has also been internationally recognized for its financial integrity.
