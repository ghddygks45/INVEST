Policy on Collection and Use of Personal Information
1. Purpose of collection of personal information: To contact a member for responding to his/her enquiries and suggestions
2. Personal information collected by Invest KOREA: Name (Korean or English), telephone number, e-mail address
3. Period of maintaining and using personal information: Until the day a member deletes his/her account.
4. Individual’s right to disagree to the provision of personal informa- tion and disadvantages that may be given upon his/her refusal: An individual reserves the right to disagree to the Policy on Collection and Use of Personal Information. However, the individual cannot use the online consulting service unless he/she agrees to the policy.
5. Additional Information: Please do not include any personal information including resident registration number, telephone number when posting any content on Online Consulting.
