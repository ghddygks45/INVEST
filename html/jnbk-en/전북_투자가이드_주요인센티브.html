North Jeolla Province provides KRW 20 billion of cash grant to large-scale investment companies.

Requirements
  • A company should sign a memorandum of understanding with the province.
  • More than 20 regular workers since the company’s investment in the province.
  • The investment should be over KRW 1 billion.
    ※ A foreign-invested company that has already made investment in Korea should follow the investment guides for domestic companies for its relocation, establishment and expansion.
