Subsidy for Promoting Investment

Grounds for support
Regulations for Promoting Investment in Gwangju Metropolitan City and its Enforcement Rules
Requirements
  • ① Investment more than 2 billion won or more, full-time employees of 20 or more
  • ② 50 or more full-time employees
  • ③ Venture and Innobiz-certified companies with an investment of 1.5 billion won or more, and full-time employees of 15 or more
  • ④ Headquarters, research institutes, cultural industries with an investment of 1 billion won or more, and full-time employees of 10 or more
※ Number of new full-time employees of companies within the jurisdiction: 10% of the number of full-time employees in the previous business establishment
Support details
Location subsidy, facility investment subsidy, employment, education and training subsidy, subsidy for moving employees
Support details : Classification, General, Venture, Innobiz, mining, components and materials, nanotechnology, industrial design, energy, Headquarter, research institute, culture industry, and information and communication

Employment subsidy If the number of full-time employees exceeds 20 → 600,000 won per exceeding employee per month (up to 12 months)

Education and training
subsidy		 If the number of full-time employees exceeds 20 → 600,000 won per exceeding employee per month (up to 6 months)
