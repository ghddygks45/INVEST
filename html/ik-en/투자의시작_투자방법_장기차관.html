Foreign Investment Flowchart

  1. Conclude contract
  2. Send notification of foreign investment (foreign exchange bank, currency carry-in through customs)
  3. Remit loans
  4. Deposit loans to a corporate bank account of foreign-invested company
Conclude Contract
Lenders and foreign-invested companies make contracts for long-term loans.
Notification of Foreign Investment
A foreign investor is required to send pre-notification of the foreign investment to either KOTRA (via the Foreign Investor Support Center in KOTRA headquarters or an overseas investment hub office) or a foreign exchange bank.
Remittance of Loans
The lender remits loans to a corporate bank account of foreign-invested company.
Deposit
The loan amount from the lender is deposited into the corporate bank account of the foreign-invested company in accordance with the foreign investment notification.
