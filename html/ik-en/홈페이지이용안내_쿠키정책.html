Matters Concerning the Installation, Operation, and Rejection of Devices that Automatically Collect Personal Information

KOTRA’s Invest KOREA uses cookies for the smooth operation of its website. The cookies to be utilized are limited to basic settings or devices of users. Information contained in the cookies does not directly identify the user.

As this service respects your rights to personal information, you may choose not to allow cookies. Blocking some types of cookies, however, may affect the services we provide. Blocking some types of cookies, however, may affect the services we provide. Options can be set in your web browser to allow all cookies, check and confirm each time a cookie is saved, or refuse to save any cookies.

If you refuse the installation of cookies, there may be difficulties in using website services that require log-on.

How to Set the Options
  • Internet Explorer : Tools menu at the top of the web browser ▷ Internet options ▷ Personal information ▷ Cookie blocking level settings
  • Chrome : Settings menu on the right side of the web browser ▷ Advanced settings display at the bottom of the screen ▷Content settings for personal information ▷Cookie blocking level settings

Cookie Type and Acceptance Settings

Necessary Cookies
Always Active

Necessary cookies perform various necessary activities to navigate the website and use its features. These cookies are essential for the website to function and are used only in response to service requests, such as log-in or filling out a form.

You can set your web browser to block cookies or send a warning, but some functions of our website may no longer work.

These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information and will be deleted when the web browser is closed.

Analytical cookies

Analytical cookies help to improve the website by sending information to Invest KOREA about how users use the website. These cookies collect information about how users use our website and which pages they visit the most. For example, analytical cookies enable us to count the number of website visitors and their navigation within the website.

Through this, we continuously improve the environment and performance for users, such as providing better search functions and shortening loading times.

These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information and will be deleted from the server after 30 days.

※ This is a public service and no personal information is collected that could be used for personal analysis or marketing.
