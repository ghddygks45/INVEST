Local governments may offer education and training subsidies and employment subsidies to foreign-invested companies in order to promote foreign investment, in accordance with ordinances of the local governments.
In such cases, the central government can provide financial funds to the local governments.
※ Related laws : Article 14.1 of the Foreign Investment Promotion Act, Article 20.1 of the Enforcement Decree of the Act, Articles 6 and 7 of the Standard for Support of National Financial Funds to the Local Governments for Foreign Investment Promotion Activities

Requirements

Education and training subsidies and employment subsidies are provided to foreign-invested companies that have been registered for a period of less than five years and whose share of foreign investment is at least 30 percent.

Scope of Support

The central government provides the amount that the local governments intend to support, within a monthly limit of KRW 500,000 per person for up to six months.
※ Foreign-invested companies are eligible to receive up to KRW 6 million per person for up to six months (KRW 3 million from the central government and KRW 3 million the local government).
