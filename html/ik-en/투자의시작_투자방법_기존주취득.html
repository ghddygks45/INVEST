Foreign Investment Flowchart

  1. Conclude contract
  2. Send notification of foreign investment KOTRA, foreign exchange bank(immediately)
  3. Remit investment funds
  4. Registration of the Foreign-Invested Company or Registration of Alteration agency where the first notification form was filed(immediately)
Conclusion of Contract for Transfer of Stocks
A foreign investor and existing shareholders make a contract for the transfer of stocks.
Notification of Foreign Investment
A foreign investor is required to send pre-notification of the foreign investment to either KOTRA (via the Foreign Investor Support Center in KOTRA headquarters or an overseas investment hub office) or a foreign exchange bank.
Registration of the Foreign-Invested Company or Registration of Alteration
As the last step for a foreign investment, a foreign-invested company or alteration must be registered to the agency where the first notification was filed (KOTRA or a foreign exchange bank).
※ Registration of the capital increase is required when new stocks are acquired for a capital increase of a domestic corporation, but registration is not required when existing stocks are acquired.
