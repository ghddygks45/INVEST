Tax Deductions for Investment in Facilities for Commercializing New Growth Engine Technologies

The investment in facilities that aim to commercialize new growth engine technologies as prescribed by the Restriction of Special Taxation Act can be deducted from corporate tax until December 31, 2021.

Tax Reductions or Exemptions for Startups in Employment or Industrial Crisis Areas

Companies that start a designated type of business* or establish an office (except for cases of relocating an existing workplace) in areas affected by crises are subject to reductions or exemptions of corporate tax or income tax in accordance with related laws. Corporate tax and income tax will be 100% exempted for five years from the year in which the income occurs (limit varies depending on size of the company).
※ A total of 31 types of businesses including startups and small- and medium-sized companies
※ Related law: Article 99.9 of the Restriction of Special Taxation Act
