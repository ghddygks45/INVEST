Export Powerhouse Led by the Manufacturing Industry

Korea is the 7th largest exporter in the world. Exports from Korea are led by the manufacturing industry, and major items of export include semiconductors, automobiles, automobile parts, oil products, and displays. Since the worldwide outbreak of COVID-19, exports of consumer goods, medicine, and medical supplies have surged.
※ Source : KITA (2019)

Among Top 3 FTA Territories Globally

Korea is part of the world’s 3rd largest FTA territory connected with 56 FTA partner countries and economic blocs, including the EU, the US, and China.
Foreign-invested companies located in Korea can enter most developed markets around the world while capitalizing on the advantages of being based in an Asian country.
※ Source : MOTIE, KITA
