산업입지에는 경제자유구역, 외국인투자지역 및 자유무역지역 등의 외국투자가를 지원하기 위한 지역이 있으며, 입지는 임대 또는 분양의 형태로 지원된다. 이러한 지역은 일정 요건 충족 시 입지지원 뿐만 아니라 조세감면이 적용되며 다른 법령에서 제한되는 사항에 대한 예외가 적용되는 경우도 있다.

The Saemangeum Development Project provides incentives for investors in various forms such as the Saemangeum Special Act and the Foreign Investment Promotion Act

Incentives for Foreign-invested Company

Saemangeum National Industrial Complex to Create New Value for Northeast Asian Economy
Incentives for Foreign-invested Company : Category, Description
Category Description
Central Government Location support
  • Qualification : FDI of USD 30 M or more
  • Benefit : 100% Rent exemption
  • Degree of support: less than 50% of investment amount by the company
Cash Grant
  • Qualification : FDI ratio of 30% or more
  • Amount Ceiling : Within 5 to 40% of FDI (Amount decided by negotiation)
Local Government General Investment
  • Location : Less than 50% of the land price
  • Cash : Maximum about USD 4 M (Jeollabuk-do and Gunsan-si respectively)
Large-scale Investment
  • Location : Less than 50% of the land price
  • Cash : Investment more than USD 83 M or employment of 300 workers /Limit :Up to USD 33 M
    ※ Location support amount will be deducted from cash grant provision amount
  • Plant Building : Maximum USD 4 M
  • Facility Investment : Maximum USD 4 M for location support
Employment USD 125 for six months per employee exceeding 20 workers / USD 0.8 M
Educational & Training USD 83 for six months per employee exceeding 20 workers / USD 0.6 M
National Tax Corporate tax 100% exemption for 5 years+50% reduction for 2years for start-ups or newly established enterprises
(Industrial Crisis Zone, by April 4, 2022)
Customs 100% exemption for 5 years(For imported capital goods reported as foreign investment)
Local tax Acquisition Tax Property Tax 100% exemption for 15 years

Incentive Case

  • Toray (2016)
    • Field : Automotive PPS resin and compound
    • Benefit : Free rental for 100 years / 100% exemption of corporate tax for 5 years, 50% reduction for 2 years
  • Solvay (2016)
    • Field : Eco-friendly tire silica
    • Benefit : Cash support/ 100% exemption of corporate tax for 5 years, 50% reduction for 2 years
  • 8 small hidden champions in the electric vehicle field (2019)
    • Field : Bus, truck, camper, etc.
    • Benefit : Subsidy/100% exemption of corporate tax for 5 years
