Strengths of Saemangeum

Saemangeum National Industrial Complex, The creator of new values for the Northeast Asian economy
Status
  • Location: 466 Saemangeumbuk-ro, Gunsan-si, Jeollabuk-do
  • Area: 1,850 ha
  • Project period: 2008-2023
  • Project implementer: Korea Rural Community Corporation
  • Licensee: Saemangeum Development Administration
  • Affordable purchase and rental prices
    • Sales : 151,249 won/㎡, USD 126/㎡
    • Lease : 1% of the officially assessed land price for 100 years (USD 1/ ㎡ per year)
  • Connectivity with nearby industrial complexes
    • Adjacent to Gunsan National Industrial Complex and 12 other industrial complexes
  • Comprehensive Bonded Area / FTA with 55 countries
    • Tariff reduction and deferment of custom duties
  • Access to excellent human resources & Cooperation with Industry-Academia
    • 10 universities, 10 specialized high schools, 28 lifelong educational institutions, 11 R&D institutions
  • Central government support and investment incentives
    • Special Eco-friendly Vehicle Regulation Zone, Gunsan small-strong special R&D zone
    • Corporate tax exemption for 5 years + 50% reduction for 2 years, long-term rental of land
