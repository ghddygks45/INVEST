Active Application for Intellectual Property Rights

The number of applications for intellectual property rights in Korea is the 4th largest in the world, following China, the US, and Japan, and Korea ranks 5th in the world in Patent Cooperation Treaty (PCT) patent applications. In addition, Korea stands on top in terms of applications compared to GDP and population, far outdistancing other leading countries, showing Korea’s excellence in application for intellectual property rights.

Global Leader in Intellectual Property Rights

Korea has strengthened international cooperation as a member of the IP5 offices that have improved the efficiency of the examination process for patents worldwide. Korea further cooperates with ASEAN and Eastern European countries, has hosted the Korea-ASEAN Heads of IP Offices Meeting and signed cooperation agreements, and signed MOUs with the UAE and Saudi Arabia for the advancement of the intellectual property field.
Korea, as a chair country of the 2019 IP5 Heads of Office with Industry Meeting, successfully led discussions on the international standard of intellectual property rights, cementing its status in the intellectual property sector. The speed of its patent process is one of Korea’s strengths in this area. The examination and processing period for patent and utility model registry in Korea is shorter than in the US (16.3 months) and similar to Japan (10.4 months).
