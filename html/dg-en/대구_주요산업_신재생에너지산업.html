Establishment of the Green Energy Cluster

Implementation of the Leading Strategic Project in Green Energy
《Photovoltaic Industry》
  • First stage (leading industry): Enhancing the global competitiveness in the photovoltaic component materials to develop crystalline Si, compound thin film, organic and next-generation solar cells (2009 - Apr. 2012, KRW 40 bn) - Projects (19 R&D, 2 corporate support), relevant companies (21 companies conducting the project, 21 companies participating in the project)
  • Second stage (leading strategic industry): Establishing the global photovoltaic production base to develop the technology for fuel-efficient, low-cost, long-lasting products and the improvement of production efficiency (May 2012, 2014, KRW 35 bn)
《Fuel Cell》
  • First stage (leading industry): Establishing a global fuel cell hub to support the development and marketing of fuel cells for generation and transport and hydrogen storage system (2009 - Apr. 2012, KRW 34.3 bn) - Projects (10 R&D, 2 corporate supports), relevant companies (12 companies conducting the projects, 37 companies participating in the projects)
  • Second stage (leading strategic industry): Establishing a global hydrogen fuel cell production base to support the development of fuel cell-related component materials and highly efficient batteries (May 2012 - 2014, KRW 30 bn)
Establishment of the Green Energy Foundation
  • Organizing the Green Energy EXPO (March, KRW 2 bn) and International LED & Display Fair (Sep. KRW 300 m) to share information on the green energy industry and facilitate marketing
    • Organizing an export seminar during the period when the Green Energy EXPO is held ( by the Ministry of Knowledge Economy)
  • Implementing the photovoltaic test bed to make components and materials commercially available through the test and evaluation of the photovoltaic products manufactured by Daegu-based companies (2011 - 2013, KRW 29.9 bn)
    • Supporting the photovoltaic products and establishing system

Green Technology Development and Growth Momentum

  • Developing solar-powered technology utilizing the tower-type solar power plant (completed in Jun. 29, 2011, KRW 11.7 bn), implementing follow-up projects and manufacturing the intelligent green house (2010 - 2014, KRW 11 bn)
    • Implementing the hydrogen production project using high temperature, attracting relevant institutions (branch of the Energy Technology Research Institute)
    • Development of solar cells and intelligent management system: 2012, KRW 2.27 bn (2 bn by national budget, 200 m by city budget, 70 m by private investment)
  • Implementing the project to make 10 kWh-grade energy storage system for household use commercially available (Samsung SDI, 2010 - 2013, KRW 27.8 bn)
    • Installing the system for 100 households (10 households in 2011, 90 households in 2012)
