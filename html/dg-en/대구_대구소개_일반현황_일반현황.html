General Status

  • Location: Daegu is located in south eastern inlands, sitting in a basin surrounded by mountains extended east and west, and low hills lying north and south. The city is 80 km away from Southeast coast and 293 km away from Seoul.
  • Area: 883.48㎢
  • Population: 2,518,000 (No. of residents living within an hour's drive: 5,220,000, No. of foreign residents: 25,203 (1%))
  • Average annual temperature: 14.0℃
  • Average annual precipitation: 1,027.4 ㎜

Industry Overview

Building on its well-established industrial infrastructure, Daegu is enhancing competitiveness by developing state-of-the-art and value-added fashion & textile industries, advancing major industries including machinery and metal, and other specialized industries, and promoting high-tech knowledge-based industries.
