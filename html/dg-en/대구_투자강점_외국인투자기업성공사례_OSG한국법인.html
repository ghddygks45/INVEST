OSG Korea Corporation

Overview
OSG Korea Corporation, Korea’s leading cutting-tool producer, has been the backbone of the domestic machinery industry that had solely depended on imports. Forging partnership with renowned cutting-tool maker OSG Japan in 1985, OSG Korea emerged as a leader in cutting-tool production. Thanks to continuous research and investment, and exchange of technologies with partners, it has become the first SME to receive Korea Quality Grand Award and acquired quality certifications from home and abroad. OSG Korea is playing a pivotal role in the development of auto makers and components companies including Hyundai, Kia, GM Daewoo, Renault Samsung, WIA, SL, and Mando and electronics companies such as Samsung Electronics and LG Electronics, producers of metal molds, bolts, and nuts as well as businesses in aerospace, shipbuilding, and heavy machinery. OSG Korea is now exporting its products to Japan, Southeast Asia, Europe, and the U.S., contributing to turning Korea into a genuine “global technology powerhouse.”

Competitiveness and Vision
OSG Korea proclaimed a vision to best serve customers as a globally competitive company in the age of unbridled competition and as a world leader in high quality products. To this end, the company is pursuing continuous innovation through various activities concerning TQEM, TPM, TPS, LEAN ·and 6Sigma. It has been sparing no effort to become a leading global company with high product and service quality, and excellent human resources
