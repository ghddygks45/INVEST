Convenient Living Environment for Foreigners

The education environment (kindergarten, middle and high school education) for foreigners has been improved with the establishment of Daegu International School, the first foreign education institute of Korea
  • Daegu International School (DIS)
    • Location: Esiapolis
      [east of Palgong-ro, Bongmu-dong, Daegu Metropolitan City (next to Yeongshin school)]
    • Area: 17,815 m2
    • Opened in: August 2010
    • No. of students: Around 580 students in elementary, middle, and high schools (plus two kindergartens)
    • Curriculum: Kindergarten, elementary, middle, and high school courses
    • Operator: Lee Academy, USA
  • Ten five-star hotels and a number of shopping centers
  • About 3,000 medical institutions
    • Pleasant natural environment surrounded by Mt. Palgong, Mt. Biseul, and the Geumho river Cultural heritages in nearby areas which enable visitors to experience the Buddhist and Confucian culture together and a number of UNESCO World Cultural Heritages and tourist attractions in Gyeongju and Andong
