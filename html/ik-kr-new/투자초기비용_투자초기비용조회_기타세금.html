This service provides tax and other data for investors to refer to when calculating initial investment cost. The related data is provided based on labor cost data from the tax base and wage standards of attorneys stated by the Korea Association of Beommusa Lawyers.

In addition to labor cost, four major insurances, and rent, you can search for basic taxes, such as corporate registration and license taxes, local education taxes, and attorney fees, by Capital and the area you wish to open and operate a business.

Please note that the exact investment amount, type of business, size, and region, etc. may differ; therefore, errors may occur or actual figures may differ. Data is for reference only.

Basis of Calculation
  • Corporation registration and license tax
    • General: Tax base stated in Article 27 of the Local Tax At * Tax rate stated in Article 28.1.6 of the same act (4/1000); In case that the tax amount is under KRW 112,500, KRW 112,500 will be imposed.
    • Overconcentration control region: Corporation registration and license tax is 300/100 of the above-mentioned rate according to Article 6.1.1 of the Seoul Metropolitan Area Readjustment Planning Act (meaning, threefold; Article 28.2 of the Local Tax Act)
    • Invested amount * 4/1000 or 12/1000 (Overconcentration control region)
  • Local education tax
    • Article 150 of the Local Tax Act
    • 20/100 of the corporation registration and license tax
  • Attorney fee
    • Compensation standard of the Korea Association of Bemmusa Lawyers
    • Fees for business/corporate registration - Refer to Article 13.1 Payment
