This service provides labor cost data for investors to refer to when calculating initial investment cost. The related data is provided based on labor cost data from the Labor Cost Information System managed(www.wage.go.kr) by the Ministry of Employment and Labor.

It is possible to search by a variety of criteria, such as industry, occupation, business size, experience, and gender. In the case of industry, occupation, and business size, estimates using a combination of two conditions can be viewed.

Please note that the labor cost level is provided based on the estimated labor cost level of the previous year; therefore, errors may occur or actual figures may differ. Data is for reference only.

Basis of Calculation
  • Based on data from the 2020 Statistics on Labor Cost Structure
  • Analyzed by connecting with the wage increase/decrease rate for each industry in the Survey on Business Labor Force, converting it to the rate of June 2021, and converting it again to the annual unit (monthly salary as of June 2021 x 12 months)
  • This is the labor cost level of full-time workers in businesses with five or more full-time workers. The agricultural, forestry, fishing, public administration, defense, and social security administration; employment within households; consumption and production activities within households that are not otherwise classified; and employees of international and foreign organizations are excluded.
