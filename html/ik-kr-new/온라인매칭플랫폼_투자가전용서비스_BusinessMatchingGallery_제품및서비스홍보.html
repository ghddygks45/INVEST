Wireless Hand Type Drug Infusion Device

Wireless handy drug injector – SB Med has developed a wireless handheld type drug injector that is essential for plastic surgery, skin treatment, and obesity procedures. It has also developed the world’s first wireless foot among similar products to maximize the user’s convenience and increase the effectiveness of surgical procedures, which in turn increases the satisfaction of the operator and patient. This product has been certified and approved and is now being mass produced.

Acquired CE MDD class II B, EU medical device certification – The company’s wireless handy type drug injector is a new product not yet available in the market. There are only three products including SB Med’s product among 200 drug injectors manufactured in Korea that have received CE MDD Class II B certification. When CE MDD certification is received, most countries including Middle Eastern countries, Russia, and Australia, except for the U.S., China, and Japan, recognize more than 80% of medical device approval requests. Therefore, the product can be easily exported to overseas markets by reducing the approval period/costs.
