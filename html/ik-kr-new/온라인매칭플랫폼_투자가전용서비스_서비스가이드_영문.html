Investment Matching Service
Invest KOREA provides automated virtual meeting with promising Korean companies.
You can find in-depth information of Korean companies at Investment Opportunities, and request meetings.

Overview

Investment Matching service is an Invest KOREA’s digital transformation project. We provide automated investment matching service, and make it simple and convenient for you to to arrange virtual meetings. You can browse Korean companies’ information, and request meetings to your potential investment targets.

Service Flow

  1. Browse Korean companies on Investment Opportunities
  2. Sign in with Investor ID
  3. Publicity information upload
  4. Review your information by your nearest KOTRA office
  5. Request meetings to potential investment targets
  6. (when request is accepted) Virtual Meeting on ZOOM
