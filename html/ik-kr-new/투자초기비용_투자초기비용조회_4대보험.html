Korea, as a primary means of social security policy, implements the social insurance system in order to protect workers and their families from threats, such as injury, disease, aging, unemployment, and death.

Korea's four major social insurance systems include ① industrial accident compensation insurance for workers' occupational accidents, ② health insurance or disease insurance for illness and injury, ③ national pension for disability, death, and aging, and ④ employment insurance for unemployment. These insurance systems aim at anticipating and responding to risks and ensuring the economic life of the people.

Employers who intend to run businesses in Korea face special circumstances in which the four major insurance costs must also be considered as part of labor costs. This service helps investors identify the four major social insurance systems when calculating initial investment costs. Data is provided based on data provided by the Social Insurance Information System (www.4insure.or.kr).

Please note that the exact business size, region, subject, and scope of insurance per business and per employee, etc. may differ; therefore, errors may occur or actual figures may differ. Data is for reference only.

Basis of Calculation of Four Major Insurances
  • National Pension
    • Annuity = Base monthly income * Annuity rate
    • The employer contribution by the employer stated in Article 88.3 of the National Pension Act
  • National Health Insurance
    • Health insurance premium = Monthly wage * Health insurance premium rate
    • Insurance contribution rates stated in Article 73.1 of the National Health Insurance Act and Article 44.1 of the Enforcement Decree of the same act
    • Long-term care insurance premium = Health insurance premium *Long-term care insurance premium rate
    • Article 9.1 of the Long-term Care Insurance Act and Article 4 of the Enforcement Decree of the same act
  • Employment Insurance
    • Employment insurance premium = Standard monthly income amount * 0.8% (unemployment benefit) + employment stability, vocational competency development contribution (standard monthly income amount * application rate according to the company size)
    • Article 14 of the Act on the Collection of Insurance Premiums for Employment Insurance and Industrial Accident Compensation Insurance and Article 12 of the Enforcement Decree of the same act
  • Industrial Accident Compensation Insurance
    • Industrial accident compensation insurance premium: Average monthly wage * Premium rate / 1000
    • Article 14 of the Act on the Collection of Insurance Premiums for Employment Insurance and Industrial Accident Compensation Insurance and Article 12 of the Enforcement Decree of the same act
