Hi-speed Transportation Network

Core infrastructure, such as expressways, railroads, and ports, perfectly connects the province with the metropolitan regions. Gangwon’s outstanding accessibility reaches major cities in Northeast Asia within two hours by plane.
  • Expanding and extending expressways
    • Construction of Dongseo Expressway (Seoul-Yangyang): Seoul-East Hongcheon line has been completed, East Hongcheon-Yangyang line will open in 2016.
    • Construction of Second Yeongdong Expressway (Gyeonggi Gwangju-Wonju): will open in 2016.
    • Construction of Donghae Expressway (Samcheok-Sokcho): will open in 2015.
  • Expansion of the railroad network
    • Dongseo High-speed Railway (Chuncheon-Sokcho) will be constructed.
    • Gyeongchun Line (Seoul-Chuncheon) Double Track Subway: high speed subway train (ITX Youth) started to run in 2012.
    • Joongang Line (Deokso-Wonju) Double Track Subway was opened.
    • Wonju-Gangneung Line Double Track Subway will be opened in 2017.
    • Donghae Joongbu Line (Pohang-Samcheok): will be opened in 2019 (part of the line was opened).
    • Construction of the suburban railway in the capital area (Seoul Sinchon-Yeoncheon Sintan-ri-Cheorwon Daema-ri): was opened.
  • Expanding and extending national highway
    • National roads No.5 (Wonju-Cheorwon).
    • National roads No.7 (Samcheok- Goseong).
    • National roads No.31 (Yeongwol-Yanggu).
    • National roads No.38 (Jecheon-Donghae) ,etc. 12 lines.
  • Air routes: 2018 Yangyang International Ariport flight plans
    • International: 16 lines (China 9, Japan 1, Russia 2, Taiwan 1, Vietnam 3)
      ※China: Beijing, Hangzhou, Harbin, etc,
      ※ Russia: Vladivostok, Khabarovsk.
    • Domestic: 4 lines (Jeju, Gimhae, Incheon, Gimpo)
      ※ Chinese tourist groups (min. 3 tourists) may stay for tourism purposes for up to 15 days without having to tranfer to Jeju Island.
  • Sea routes
    • Building up Tourism·Distribution Network (sea routes)
      • DBS Cruise Ferry sea route: Donghae·Mukho Port~Japan Sakaiminato, Russia Vladivostok.
      • Sokcho~Zarubino(Russia)·Hunchun(China) sea route: Sokcho~Zarubino·Hunchun, Zarubino~Russia Vladivostok.
    • Plan for the opening of new sea routes
      • Donghae·Mukho Port: Donghae~Japan Tsurugashima.
      • Sokcho port: Sokcho~Japan Kitakyushu.
