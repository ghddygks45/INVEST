Characteristics of Bio Industry

Bio industry is an industry to tap into the functions and information of all bio organisms of animals, plants and microbes and produce and commercialize all kinds of materials doing good to human beings utilizing ferments as catalyst.
Bio Industry is comprised of: production utilizing bio engineering technologies, such as GMO technologies, cell fusion, protein engineering, cell culture, bio-process, and the processes.
Bio engineering integrates physical, chemical, mathematical, and computational sciences and engineering principles to study bio logy, medicine, behavior, and health. It advances fundamental concepts; creates knowledge from the molecular to the organ systems levels; and develops innovative bio logics, materials, processes, implants, devices, and informatics approaches for the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of disease, for patient rehabilitation, and for improving health.

Overview of Major Bionics Technologies

Fundamental Technologies
Fundamental Technologies : GMO, Cell Fusion, Protein Engineering
Type of Technology Characteristics
GMO A technology to modify the genes of molecules by transforming the genes in vitro and injecting the transformed genes through vehicles into the bio system to produce useful materials.
Cell Fusion A technology to fuse two cells of different characteristics into one to produce a complete cell carrying the useful characteristics of each cell.
Protein Engineering A technology that motify the molecule structure of proteins that carry amino acids sequences to produce proteins for special use. It is mainly used in enhancing the safety and efficacy of proteins.
Advanced Technologies
Advanced Technologies : Fermentation, Cell Culture, Bio Process, Bio Engineering
Type of Technology Characteristics
Fermentation A Biotechnology with the longest history of culturing microbes within a fermenter with a given temperature and humidity
Cell Culture
  • Cell culturing is comprised of animal cell culturing and plant cell culturing. This technology is used in producing advanced bio active materials or GMO proteins.
  • Recently technologies in massive culture of plant tissues and cells, massive culture of insect cells and massive culture of mammal's cells.
Bio Process A process that utilizes bio system in producing advanced bio active materials and include bio-reaction engineering and separation/ purification technologies.
Bio Engineering An integrated system engineering that industrialize and put to practical use the bio logical technologies. It utilizes bio logical methods in designing and building factories.

Current Status of the Korean Bio Industry

In 1998, domestic demands in Bio Industry amounted to KRW 508.5 billion and they have continued to grow by 31.9% annually since 1992.
In 1998 Korean Bio Industry production amounted to KRW 820 billion and continued to grow by 50% each year over the past five years.

Current Status of Gangwon's Bio Industry

As most of Gangwon provice except industrial districts are designated as the water supply protection zones and factory sites are not large enough, Chuncheon and Gangwon may not be a good place to do business in manufacturing industry. Therefore, Chuncheon chose and attracted bio industry as the main engine behind regional economic growth as the industry is pollution-free and heavily dependent upon R&D.
Chuncheon Bio Venture Support Center
Recently completing the "Bio Venture Support Center" in 1999, Chuncheon is putting all its efforts and resources in laying the foundation for bio industry.
Picking bio agriculture, bio environment, bio medicine and silver bio logy as its main industries, the city encourages business operations that are in line with its bio industry policy, supports developing business items and helps venture businesses to go independent.

Central Government's Plan to Foster Bioindustry

Each ministries of Korean government set aside and invest significant amount of budgets in bio Industry. In 1999, the Ministry of Science & Technology (MOST) spent KRW 99.4 billion, Ministry of Agriculture & Forestry (MAF) KRW 28 billion. And out of the combined KRW 160.8 billion from the two ministries, KRW 145 billion went to R&D and KRW 15.8 billion. was invested in supporting facilities and infrastructures.
Taking the current situation in the industry that lacks fundamentals, the Ministry of Commerce, Industry & Energy (MOCIE), for its part, plans to place its resources in enhancing infrastructure like venture support center and Korea Environment & Merchandise Testing Institute (KEMTI).

  • Establishing a bio-specific investment association supported by government, venture investment and bioindustry, bio support fund-raising and encouraging bio-specific investment association.
  • Strengthening workforce in appraisal of bio ventures and bio venture business establishment sessions, establishing start-up supporting booth and mending regulations and tax benefit systems.
Improved Bio Industry Support System
  • Enacting relative regulations like a tentatively named "Bioindustry Development Base Building Act" to establish the supporting system.
  • Separately establishing a "Bioindustry Development" part in the "Industrial Fundamental Technology Development Project"
  • Building a efficient and well-organized cooperative among MOST (fundamental R&D), MOCIE (commercialization, applied technology, infrastructure), MAF, MOHW, ME, MOMAF and MOE
Enhanced Comunity Recognition of Bio Industry
  • Launching “Bioindustry Award” and PR activities on bio R&D and industry including domestic bio industry, R&D centers and supporting agencies.
  • Holding bio industry exhibition, developing cyber education programs and launching a bio society civil council for cooperation between government and private sector.

uilding and Clustering Bio Industry Regional Innovation Base

Establishing Technology Innovation System through Competition and Cooperation
Maximizing Research Results through Competition and Cooperation among Industry, Academia, Research, and Administration
  • Encouraging cooperation among bio businesses and small-and-medium businesses/ventures, and building network system of bio research equipments.

Efforts towards International Technical Cooperation and Foreign Investment Inducement
  • Expanding international cooperation with advanced bio industries of the U.S.A. and Japan and attracting foreign invesments.
  • As for technologies that are hard to develop within the country, encouraging joint R&D with overseas research institutions and efficiently coping with the changes in bio-related international conventions.
Expanded Governmental Support for Technical Innovation in Bio Industry
  • The Korean government has kept increasing the support ratio for bio industry out of its R&D budgets by 1% each year, eventually expanding the share from 5.4% in 1999 to more than 10% in 2005.

Requirements for Nurturing Bioindustry

Industrial-Academic Cooperation on R&D
  • The source of competitiveness in bio industry comes from whether one has specific technology. Therefore, in need is a research cooperation function that can connect the R&D capability of universities and commercizlizing capability of businesses.
  • Especially in the regions that lacks bio industrial fundamentals, a networking system of R&D supporting projects and commercialization will help regional bio industry to prosper.
Brain-training
A sustainable bio industry requires brain pool and re-education system.
Appraisal & Approval Agencies
  • Saftey approval agencies are necesary for domestic bio products to advance into overseas markets.
  • Specifically in the fields of bio agriculture and bio enviroment, appraisal and approval agencies are required in Gangwon.
BioIndustrial Complex
  • Spaces are provided to the companies that grow out of the Bio Venture Support Center and relocate in the Complex and continue business activities of production/manufacturing.
  • Gangwon Bio Industrial Complex shall form the environment where bio venture companies can stablize and grow into manufacturing companies, while fueling regional economy by nurturing bio industry in Gangwon.
Support for Bio Venture Start-ups
Establishing venture incubating system and bio-related administrative service system for business start-ups in the laboratories or spin-offs
