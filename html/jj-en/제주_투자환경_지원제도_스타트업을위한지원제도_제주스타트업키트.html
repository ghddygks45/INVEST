Future Strategy & Startup Team of Future Strategies Bureau, Jeju Special Self-Governing Province

The Startup Team of Future Strategies Bureau at Jeju Special Self-Governing Province promotes regulatory innovation for startups and develops an ecosystem for nurturing new industries.
  • Investment in and promotion of high-tech startups through operation of the Fourth Industrial Revolution Strategic Fund
  • Investment in agricultural, fishery, and livestock startups for promotion of primary industries
  • Operation of a variety of business incubation programs, etc.

Jeju Creative Economy Innovation Center

Jeju’s first startup support institution which aims to identify and promote outstanding startups through local customized programs.
  • Facility size : 3/4F, Jeju Venture-maru Bldg. (1,924m2)
    • 3rd floor: group workspace for exchange and networking, workspace dedicated for visitors, meeting rooms, lecture rooms, training space, counseling offices for one-stop legal and financial services, etc.
    • 4th floor: residency area (45 seats), space dedicated for Post-BI companies
  • Targets for support
    • Would-be startups or startups three years old or less
  • Support content
    • The Edge program (6 months), Post-BI space (within a year), recommendation of a seed money investment project, basic support and special support for startups in incubation, provision of workspace, common use space, etc. for domestic and foreign startups
    • Support of stay expenses for resident businesses (within a month, up to 5 people), provision of registration transfer fees for Jeju headquarters
    • Address: 217, Jungang-ro, Jeju City, Jeju Special Self-Governing Province
    • Website : http://jccei.kr

STARTUP BAY

Startup Bay is a Seogwipo-based startup support organization which identifies and promotes outstanding startups specialized in the culture, tourism, agriculture, marine and ICT industries as well as other local industries in the island’s southern city.
  • Facility size
    • 2nd floor: co-working space (for networking and training)
    • 3rd floor: resident offices, seminar rooms, meeting rooms
  • Support
    • 20 resident businesses, 1 year of residency
  • Support content
    • Workspace (rent and maintenance fees exempted), training and mentoring programs
  • Support programs
    • Multiple phases of the incubating and accelerating process
    • Stay support programs for attracting outstanding (potential) startups from home and abroad
    • Digital nomad programs (50% for startup training, 30% for local tours, 20% for digital nomad day)
    • Initial startup package (commercialization funds of up to KRW 100 million)
    • Hosting of the Startup Ecosystem Conference, local startup capacity building projects, identification and promotion of early startups
    • Tel : +82 64 732-0049
    • Address: 3F, 86, Jungjeong-ro, Seogwipo City

W360

W360 was formerly an outdated meteorological agency building which has been remodeled into an innovative incubation space for startup and growth support infrastructure.
  • Facility size : 959㎡
    • Co-working space, lecture rooms, meeting rooms, resident space (26 seats), video content studio, space for short-term project operators, etc.
  • Targets for support
    • Would-be startups or startups seven years old or less, which are run by individual or corporate business owners who possess big data or blockchain technologies, or who are intending to provide relevant services using such technologies.
  • Support content
    • Residency programs (2 months), recommendation of a seed money investment project, basic support and special support for startups in incubation, provision of workspace, common use space, etc. for domestic and foreign startups
    • Support of stay expenses for residents (within a month, up to 3 people), provision of registration transfer fees for Jeju headquarters
    • Address: 13-1, Dongmun-ro 9-gil, Jeju City, Jeju Special Self-Governing Province
    • Website : http://jccei.kr/wspace/intro.htm
  • W-SPACE : a co-working space available to anyone
  • Alpha(α) : auditorium where a variety of training programs and events take place
  • Delta (δ) and Epsilon (ε) rooms : meeting rooms freely available through reservation
  • Beta (β) room : workspace available to residents
  • Studio Room : studio for video content creators
  • Gamma (γ) room : project room for short-term project operators

JDC Jeju Innovation Growth Center

Jeju Innovation Growth Center drives the innovative growth of Jeju Island with the two-track approach of promoting startups in the ICT field and promoting industries in the autonomous/EV field.
  • Facility size
    • 34 offices and 18 meeting rooms
  • Support
    • Selection of resident businesses through a once-a-year evaluation process (additional selection is available when necessary) and the provision of one-year incubation benefits
  • Support contents
    • Free provision of residency programs and workspace
    • Opportunities to participate in the resident support project for Jeju Free International City Development Center‘s Science Park
    • A wide range of programs, including special lectures of experts, mentoring, consulting, support for attracting investment, demodays, and networking events in the center
    • Tel : +82 64 797-5764, 5525
    • Email : sweetrain@jdcenter.com / hig@jdcenter.com
    • Address: Jeju Innovation Growth Center, Semiyang Building, 330, Cheomdan-ro, Jeju City
