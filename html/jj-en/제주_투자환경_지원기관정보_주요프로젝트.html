Key Projects of Jeju Free International City Development Center

Jeju Science Park
  • Jeju’s only national industrial complex which hosts ICT and biotechnology businesses
  • Business complex which provides an ideal business environment, eased regulations, and a variety of incentives
  • Area
    1,098,878 ㎡
  • Number of occupants
    126
  • Number of employees
    2,230 person (as of 2018)
  • Annual revenue
    KRW 2,974.6 billion (as of 2018)
  • Key facilities
    • IT/BT businesses
    • public/private research institutes
    • Residential complex, etc.
  • Jeju Science Park Website : https://www.jeju-sp.com

  • Area

  • Key facilities

Jeju Healing Hills
  • Asia’s best medical complex combining Jeju’s pristine natural environment and medical tourism services
  • Area
    1,539,339 ㎡

Jeju Myths and History Theme Park

  • Area
    3,985,601 ㎡
  • Key facilities
