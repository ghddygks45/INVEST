Jeju Science Park

Objectives
To lay the foundation for promoting new industries in Jeju by developing knowledge-based industrial complexes in the fields of ICT and biotechnology
Location
Yeongpyeong-dong, Jeju City
Area
1,099,000㎡
Project Budget
KRW 580 billion
Designation / Development Method
National industrial complex / Public development method
Types of Businesses Attracted
IT/BT businesses, public/private research institutes, etc.
Major Achievements
  • Activated Jeju Science Park by completing the sale of industrial facility sites and attracting about 150 resident businesses
  • Sales of occupants: KRW 2,974.6 billion (as of 2018)
  • Employees: about 2,200 persons
Location Conditions
  • Jeju City Hall, Jeju International Airport, Jeju Port, etc. are located within a 10km radius.
  • Human resources and industrial linkage are easily available through neighboring higher education institutions, etc.
  • Number of employees : 2,200 person
